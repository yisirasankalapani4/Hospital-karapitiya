About Karapitiya Maternity Hospital

Known as the Helmut Kohl Memorial Maternity Hospital, our facility represents the pinnacle of maternal healthcare in South Asia. Located in Galle, Southern Province of Sri Lanka, we are part of the prestigious Teaching Hospital Karapitiya complex.

With state-of-the-art medical equipment and internationally trained healthcare professionals, we provide comprehensive maternal and infant care services. Our hospital serves as a teaching facility, contributing to medical education and research in obstetrics and gynecology.

Key Achievements:

  • South Asia's largest maternity hospital
  • 400+ bed capacity with modern facilities
  • International funding and partnership with Germany
  • Teaching hospital status with medical education programs
  • Serves the entire Southern Province of Sri Lanka
